About this site

I've registered the domain www.never-land.de some time in 1998 or 1999. First, it served as my personal, digital & hybrid outlet before I purchased the domain with my own name in 2002. It all happened around the same time where like-minded enthusiasts of the band started to pull up their webpages like www.poison-door.net or www.myheartland.co.uk. In absence of social media, one would refer to specialised forums and email lists to get the lastest information and discussion. This site now is a pure hobby project of mine where I share contents and collectibles about That Band Above The Chemist.

About The Sisters Of Mercy

This website is not officially endorsed. It is purely a short expression of gratitude and dedication to the works of the band. The official website with lots and lots of words is available on www.thesistersofmercy.com. Chairman Bax presumably finds his occasional outlet as @tsomofficial on Twitter/x if he has something to tell us at all. People still cannot link to BaxCorp.

Ever forward

The following text is a description by the band themselves from early 2019. Since then, there has been a rollercoaster. Dave Creffield played with the Doktor. Dylan Smith picked up the guitar in 2019 and left the stage with no guitar in 2023. Chris Catalyst moved to the desk while Sister Kai picked up the guitar in late 2023. Prior to this, the band described themselves like this:

It howls.
It’s grindingly beautiful. 
It’s dangerously sentient. 
And it’s heading your way. 

The Sisters‘ fun juggernaut drives on through 2019, with a little new chrome and a lot of vintage attitude. 

It still comes from northern England. 
It still sounds like supercharged industrial psychedelia. 

We have some old songs, some new songs, a few blinding lights and very many smoke machines. 

People seem to like the colours. Or the energy. Or something. 

Metal Hammer says, „The Sisters Of Mercy are … a lean glittering groove machine for the New Millennium.“ 

That’s not going to stop us. 

The Sisters Of Mercy are: 

    Chris Catalyst – guitars
    Ben Christo – guitars
    Andrew Eldritch – vocals
    Nurse – nurse to the Doktor
    Doktor Avalanche – drum and bass

Chris Catalyst eats daleks, so he plays the guitar. 
Ben Christo loves you, so he plays the guitar. 
Andrew Eldritch would like to believe in redemption, so he sings the songs very quietly. 
Nurse is the right hand Of God. 
Doktor Avalanche is God.
